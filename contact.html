Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins! However, we recommend making a reservation to ensure table availability, especially during peak hours and weekends.

Do you offer private dining or event space?

Absolutely! We have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests. Please contact us at catering@savorybites.com for more details.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and food allergies. Please inform your server or mention it in your reservation.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests. Street parking is also available nearby.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for purchase in any amount. Please contact us or visit the restaurant to purchase.

What's your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. This allows us to accommodate other guests waiting for reservations.
